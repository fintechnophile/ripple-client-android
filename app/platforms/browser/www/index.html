Important Safety Notes

All XRPs and IOUs are stored on the Ripple Network and not your device. InstantRipple.com does not have access to your username or password. If you lose your information you will lose your XRPs and IOUs.

Payments are irreversible. If you send XRPs or IOUs to an unknown party consider it a permanent transaction.

Keep your mobile device safe. Only install apps you fully trust. Malicious apps can steal your information and have full access to your device.

Reduce your risk by having two wallets. One wallet for daily usage and other as a savings account. Only transfer small amounts into your daily usage wallet as needed.
